Add GengoApp to Your iPhone Homescreen (iOS)

Quickly connect to your GengoApp content in 3 easy steps!

Step 1

Click on the icon at the bottom of your Safari browser.

Step 2

Find and tap on the "Add to Home Screen" icon.

Step 3

Click "Add" in the top right hand corner.

Done!

GengoApp will now be just a tap a way on your iPhone homescreen!
