Frequently Asked Questions

GengoApp is useful web-based tool for enhancing your language studies! Here are answers to some frequently asked questions.

What are Phrases?

Phrases are a place for you to upload new words or phrases that you have learned and want to remember. Click 'edit' to add notes or update the text in any way you like.

Want to access GengoApp more quickly from your iPhone homescreen? Learn how with these 3 easy steps!

What are Mistakes?

This list is a place for you to keep track of mistakes you have made when speaking or writing Japanese. By making a note of a mistake you have made, and what the correct grammar is, you can dramatically improve your speaking and writing skills.

There is also a counter function you can make use of here to keep track of how many times you've made the same mistake. This will give you useful insight into how long it typically takes you to break the habit of using some grammar incorrectly!

What are Posts?

Posts are the perfect place to practice writing. Add a photo to make your post more memorable, and include a translation of your post to strengthen your language skills!

Can others see my posts?

Currently only you are able to see the content you post. However, in the future I hope to develop the feature of adding friends so that you can get comments and feedback from native speakers!

How to delete my account?

To delete your account, please send a request via the Feedback page. Type 'Delete account' in the message area and choose 'Delete Account' from the categories dropdown. This is a permanent action, and all the content you've created along with your user info will be deleted. Processing may take up to 24 hours.
